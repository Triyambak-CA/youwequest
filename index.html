About the Firm

Built by practitioners,
driven by purpose.

YouWe Quest LLP is one of the leading providers of financial and business advisory services, established in 2021 in Delhi. We bring together deep technical expertise in statutory audit, tax advisory, debt syndication, subsidy liaison, and international taxation under one roof.

The firm was set up by a group of energetic, highly skilled professionals who have gained experience from top consulting companies. We combine the rigour of a large firm with the agility and personalised attention of a boutique practice.

Our work spans domestic and international clients — from early-stage startups seeking valuation advisory, to established corporates navigating complex tax litigation and cross-border compliance.
